A1 - How do citizens, civic ideals and government institutions interact to balance the needs of individuals and the common good?

Citizens, civic ideals, and government institutions interact to balance the needs of individuals and the common good in the objective of retaining order. All three of the Great Triumvirate attempted to keep the country together. They all wished for the country to remain as one and that is how they balanced the power of the government and the needs of the people to abolish slavery.
